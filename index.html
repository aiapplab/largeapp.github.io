Introduction

I am currently a senior engineer in School of Computer Science and Engineering of South China University of Technology (SCUT).Before joining School of Computer Science and Engineering in September 2013, I was a Senior Engineer in Information Network Engineering and Research Center (South China Network Center of CERNET) at SCUT. I was a faculty member of Guangdong Key Laboratory of Communication and Computer Network at SCUT since 2004.

My research interest lies in Big Data, Machine Learning, Brain Informatics, Computational Advertising and Recommendation, Computer Networking and Security.

I received my Ph.D from School of Computer Science and Engineering at SCUT in 2012. Before that, I received my master degree from School of Electronics and Information at SCUT in 2004, and bachelor degree from School of Electronics and Information at SCUT in 1999. I was a visiting scholar at Pennsylvania State University from 2016 to 2017.
