Introduction

Dr. Jinlong Hu is currently a senior engineer in the School of Computer Science and Engineering at South China University of Technology (SCUT). Before joining School of Computer Science and Engineering in September 2013, he was a senior engineer in Information Network Engineering and Research Center (South China Network Center of CERNET) at SCUT. He joined SCUT in 2004. He was also a faculty member of Guangdong Key Laboratory of Communication and Computer Network at SCUT from 2004. He was a visiting scholar at Pennsylvania State University from 2016 to 2017.

He received Ph.D from School of Computer Science and Engineering at SCUT in 2012, Master Degree from School of Electronics and Information at SCUT in 2004, and Bachelor Degree from School of Electronics and Information at SCUT in 1999.

He has hosted or participated over 20 research projects including the Scientific Research Joint Funds of Ministry of Education and China Mobile, the Science and Technology Planning Project of Guangdong Province, the 863 program project, the CNGI Project and etc. He holds several IT professional certifications including CISSP from 2007, Senior Programmer from 2002 and etc. He has converted many original research ideas into research prototype systems and usually into commercial products.

Research Interests

  • Big Data Analytics, Machine Learning, Fraud Detection, Brain Informatics, Computational Advertising & Recommendation, Health Informatics, Computer Networking & Security
